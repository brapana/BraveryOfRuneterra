About

Why would anyone make this?

While there are several "bravery"-type websites in existence for League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics (my other favorite Riot Games), I felt like there was a troubling lack of such a website for Legends of Runeterra. With a sizeable amount of downtime due to quarantine, creating Bravery of Runeterra was not only a great opportunity to add another project to my Software Engineering resume, but to create something fun for both myself and the Legends of Runeterra community.

I don't own that many cards!

While I realize that needing to own cards may introduce a bar of entry for using Bravery of Runeterra, I hope that players with sizeable card collections can still find joy in taking some randomized decks for a spin. That said, I would love to hear your suggestions on how to lower this bar and I am looking at future options such as narrowing card selection to cards owned by the player when such API endpoints are made available.

Who would do this?

I am a Master of Software Engineering student at the University of California, Irvine currently seeking Summer 2021 internships as well as full-time opportunities in the US (please contact me via the methods below if you have any opportunities!). I have been playing Legends of Runeterra since the beta, and I absolutely love what it has done with the digital card game formula. It's the first game I've managed to reach Diamond in after all! I am also a big fan of Riot's other games League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics, the former which I have been playing since 2013.

Questions, Suggestions, etc.

Please feel free to contact me using any of the methods below regarding Bravery of Runeterra.
