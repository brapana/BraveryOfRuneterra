About

Why would anyone make this?

While there are several "bravery"-type websites for League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics (my other favorite Riot Games), I was disappointed in the lack of variety for Legends of Runeterra. I sought a fast way to churn out bad decks for the regions I wanted and was let down by the few options I could find. In the spirit of resolving this (as well as quarantine boredom), I decided to make one myself!


What if I don't own that many cards?

While I realize that needing to own cards may introduce a bar of entry for using Bravery of Runeterra, I hope that players with sizeable card collections can still find joy in taking some randomized decks for a spin. That said, I would love to hear your suggestions on lowering this bar! I'm currently looking at filtering decks to player-owned cards when Riot lets developers like me access card collections.
